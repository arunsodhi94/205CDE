Saido Berahino: England call-up all down to my mum's sacrifice

"My mum and I have had a long journey," said the 21-year-old. "She has always been there for me and sometimes been tough with me to get me back on track..

"We have come from nothing. I have got to where I have because of her help." Berahino could make his debut in the Euro 2016 qualifier with Slovenia at Wembley on 15 November. England face Scotland in a friendly three days later. The frontman, who has scored 10 goals in 13 England Under-21 appearances, added: "England gave me a second chance in life and for me to be able to play for them is an incredible feeling. "I think I have shown I am a good player. I've a long way to go and a lot to learn as a young player, but I back myself to keep improving with each game. Berahino has scored seven league goals already this season, that is a career best for him.

Two Romanian players receive 16-match bans

Two Romanian players have been banned for 16 matches, the Romanian Football Federation has announced.

"Viitorul Constanta's Alin Seroni has been punished for a challenge that left Universitatea Craiova's Nicusor Bancu with a fractured ankle. Midfielder Gabriel Muresan, who plays for Targu Mures, will also have to sit out 16 matches after a violent tackle.
